APA Quant | Systematic Multi-Asset Allocation Framework

No forecasts. No narratives. Only systematic allocation.

APA Quant is a systematic allocation framework designed to generate structured, rule-based portfolio exposure across global asset classes. The model integrates cross-asset signals, volatility targeting, and risk overlays to adapt positioning dynamically across various market regimes.

🏛️ Strategy Philosophy
Developed for professional investors, allocators, and family offices, APA Quant provides a transparent and repeatable approach to multi-asset portfolio construction. All allocation decisions are driven by predefined, observable conditions derived from price data, volatility regimes, and intermarket correlation signals.
Systematic Framework: Fully rule-based decision architecture. Zero discretionary intervention at any stage.
Cross-Asset Signals: Model outputs across equities (US and European), commodities, global indices, and FX.
Multi-Asset Universe: Diversified exposure designed to reduce dependency on single market dynamics.
Risk Structuring: Volatility is the primary input. Position sizing is dynamically adjusted to maintain a constant risk profile.
